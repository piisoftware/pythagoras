Pythagoras of Cascadia

Surviving the Robot Apocalypse

Like troubadours of old, Pythagoras of Cascadia, in his series of evolving, iterating, timely one-man shows "Surviving the Robot Apocalypse," combines storytelling and song to contemplate moments, now and throughout history, when the world changed forever, what people did about it, and how we might come together again today to do the same. Pythagoras roams from prehistoric land formations to ancient cities, from early civilization to his own childhood, from long ago to the urgent present to always. He models his namesake to balance the rational with the irrational and determine what brings people together and what drives them apart.
